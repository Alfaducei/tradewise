I built TradeWise because I was frustrated with investment education apps that charge $40/month to explain what RSI means.

TradeWise does something different — it **acts** like a trading assistant:
- AI (Claude) analyzes your watchlist every 30 min

## Maker comment follow-ups (reply to these common questions)

**"Is it really free?"**
Yes, completely. No trial, no credit card, no premium tier ever. The only revenue is voluntary donations from people who make money using it. Open source too: [GitHub link]

**"What about financial advice disclaimers?"**
TradeWise is explicitly NOT financial advice — every signal card says so. It's a practice tool, like a flight simulator for trading. Paper mode means zero real money at risk.

**"Can it use real money?"**
Yes — there's a live mode that connects to your Alpaca live account. It's off by default, requires a confirmation dialog with full risk warnings, and you still approve every single trade.

**"Why track Congress trades?"**
Under the STOCK Act of 2012, members of Congress must publicly disclose all stock trades within 45 days. This data is freely available to every citizen — we just make it easy to see what they're buying and run AI analysis on those same stocks.
